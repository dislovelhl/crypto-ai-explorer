## 🚀 1. Executive Summary

**Product:** CryptoAI Treasury & Governance Copilot
**One-Line Pitch:** An AI-native execution layer for delegates, DAOs, and crypto builders that turns 19,000+ data points (governance forums, DeFi yields, treasury stats, grants) into an automated, risk-adjusted opportunity radar with x402 payment rails.

**The Problem:**
Information asymmetry in crypto costs millions of dollars daily.
- Delegates cannot read 50 forum posts a day to track governance risks.
- DAO treasuries leave billions of stablecoins earning 0% yield ($7.5B+ tracked across 545 DAOs).
- Builders miss $100K+ in grants because the information is too fragmented to track.

**The Solution:**
A suite of autonomous Python agents that aggregate, score, and act upon decentralized data. We have built:
1. **The Scanners**: Monitor Arbitrum/Optimism governance, 19,200+ DefiLlama pools, AI token markets, and Superteam grants.
2. **The Intelligence Layer**: A dynamic dashboard that ranks the highest Expected Value (EV) actions daily (e.g., "$10.5K grant with 0 submissions", "10.5% APY on Base USDC").
3. **The x402 Agent API**: A monetized FastAPI backend. Other AI agents pay $0.05-$0.15 in USDC via L402 macaroon proofs to access our intelligence.
