## Project: CryptoAI Explorer — Open-Source Governance Intelligence Toolkit

### Summary

CryptoAI Explorer is an open-source toolkit that provides AI-powered governance intelligence for DAOs. It monitors governance forums, analyzes proposals, tracks DeFi yields, and surfaces actionable opportunities — all through free public APIs.

The project serves the public good by:
1. **Reducing governance friction** — AI summaries help delegates participate more effectively
2. **Democratizing access** — Free tools that anyone can run, not locked behind enterprise paywalls
3. **Improving transparency** — Automated tracking of grant outcomes and treasury management
4. **Cross-DAO intelligence** — Comparative analytics across Ethereum ecosystem DAOs
