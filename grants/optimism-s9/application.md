## Project: OpGovIntel — AI Governance Intelligence for the Optimism Collective

### Summary

OpGovIntel provides AI-powered governance intelligence for Optimism delegates and community members. By automatically monitoring forum discussions, summarizing proposals, tracking grant outcomes, and analyzing delegate participation, we reduce the cognitive load on delegates and improve governance participation quality.

### Alignment with Optimism Values

| Optimism Value | How We Align |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Public goods** | Open-source toolkit, freely available |
| **Governance participation** | Reduces barriers to informed participation |
| **Transparency** | Automated tracking of grant outcomes |
| **Collective intelligence** | AI-augmented collective decision-making |

### What We Deliver

1. **Daily Optimism Governance Digest** — Automated summaries of all active proposals and forum discussions
2. **Delegate Participation Analytics** — Track which delegates are active, their voting patterns, and engagement levels
3. **Grant Outcome Tracker** — Automated monitoring of funded projects against stated milestones
4. **Proposal Risk Scoring** — AI-generated risk assessments for governance proposals

- Optimism Forum API integration (verified working with `gov.optimism.io`)

- AI analysis pipeline for proposal summarization
- Telegram bot for distribution
